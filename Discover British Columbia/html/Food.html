Iconic food in British Columbia

Japadog

When visitors come to British Columbia, they cannot miss the iconic food in there. This is a picture of this:

If visitors do not have enough time in order to explore Victoria, British Columbia, or visitors want to eat a small snack, so visitors can visit some restaurant in Food Car Street. Therefore, they can take one japadog. As a result, when tourists go from all over the world to British Columbia, they can bring one Japadog, which is a symbol of Victoria, British Columbia. Actually, Japadog is as known as Japanese hot dog because visitors knew it, and the Japanese remade it and named it Japadog. that visitors should try to find it and enjoy it in Victoria, British Columbia.

Dungeness Crab

Another iconic food in British Columbia is Dungeness Crab because British Columbia is near the sea, so that is the reason why the seafood in there is so fresh. Therefore, if visitors are big fans of the fresh seafood, they will not miss tasty food on their trip.

Being a coastal province, that the reason why a lot of visitors can visit British Columbia,fresh crab always available in there, so visitors can enjoy Dungeness Crab dishes at restaurant Sandbar, Blue Water Cafe, or Sun Sui Wah, where visitors want to try Asian taste.
